Key Findings

What we found across the assessment

Critical Gap
Lead generation pipeline is undeveloped
No structured lead generation strategy exists; there is an absence of automated outreach to cold or warm prospects entering the pipeline. With the primary goal focused on reducing churn, top-of-funnel acquisition has been deprioritized.
Critical Gap
Revenue per member lacks upsell architecture
No evidence of structured upsell or upgrade pathways; revenue per member may plateau without intentional offer architecture layered into the member journey. Untapped potential in add-ons and tiered memberships remains unrealized.
Critical Gap
Pricing structure lacks tiers and upgrade paths
Lack of tiered pricing, upgrade paths, or urgency-driven offer structures limits the ability to increase average transaction value at the point of sale. A standard single-tier model caps revenue per prospect.
Strength

Establish a Dedicated Sales Role or Structured Sales Process
Front desk staff handling sales is the most limiting structural issue identified in this diagnostic. Creating a defined sales role or assigning dedicated sales ownership with a scripted, repeatable process will immediately improve conversion consistency and free front desk staff to focus on member experience. RevAgentic can design the full intake process, build the sales playbook, and train the responsible staff member.

A 5–30 minute response time leaves significant value on the table — the first 5 minutes are critical for capturing a prospect's attention before they explore competitors. Setting up an automated SMS response the moment a lead comes in, followed by a personalized 3-touch nurture sequence, will hold prospects in the pipeline until staff can engage. RevAgentic can configure the full automation within Glofox or a connected SMS platform.
